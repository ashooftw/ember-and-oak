Our Story

One hearth. No shortcuts.

Ember & Oak started with a single question: what happens when you strip a kitchen down to fire, wood, and good ingredients? No induction, no shortcuts — just oak logs burning since before service starts, and a menu built entirely around what that fire can do. The char on the bread, the smoke in the butter, the crust on the steak: none of it is added later. It all comes from the same flame.
